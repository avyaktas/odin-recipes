Garlic Herb Butter Steak with Roasted Potatoes

Description

A restaurant-quality main course for two made easy at home. Juicy, pan-seared steak is basted with melted garlic herb butter for rich flavor and a golden crust, paired with perfectly roasted baby potatoes seasoned with rosemary. It’s hearty, flavorful, and beautifully plated — ideal for impressing guests or enjoying a gourmet night in.

Ingredients

For the Potatoes

  • 1 lb baby potatoes (halved)
  • 1 tbsp olive oi
  • Salt, pepper, and dried rosemary

For the Steak

  • 2 ribeye or strip steaks (~1 inch thick)
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 garlic cloves (crushed)
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary or thyme

Steps

Roasted Potatoes

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Toss halved potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, and rosemary.
  3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 25–30 mins, flipping halfway.

Steak

  1. Pat steaks dry and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add olive oil.
  3. Sear steaks for 3–4 minutes per side.
  4. In the final minute, reduce heat to medium and add butter, garlic, and herbs.
  5. Baste the steak with the melted herb butter for 1 minute.
  6. Remove and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Optional: Add green beans or asparagus sautéed in leftover garlic butter.
