Molten Chocolate Lava Cake with Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

A show-stopping dessert that looks impressive but is secretly simple. Rich, warm chocolate cake with a gooey molten center is baked to perfection and served with a scoop of cold, creamy vanilla ice cream. The contrast of hot and cold, soft and smooth, makes every bite unforgettable — the perfect sweet finish to a gourmet meal.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 4 oz semi-sweet chocolate (chopped or chips)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Butter and cocoa powder (for greasing ramekins)
  • Vanilla ice cream (store-bought or homemade)
  • Optional: shaved chocolate or powdered sugar for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust lightly with cocoa powder.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate and butter together in 30-second bursts, stirring until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick and pale (about 1 minute).
  5. Stir the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture.
  6. Gently fold in the flour until fully incorporated.
  7. Divide the batter evenly between the ramekins.
  8. Bake for 12–13 minutes , until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  9. Let sit for 1 minute, then carefully invert each ramekin onto a plate.
  10. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and garnish as desired. Serve immediately.
